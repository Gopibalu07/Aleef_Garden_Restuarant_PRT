Our Story

Rooted in Flavour,
Grown with Love

Aleef Garden has been Chennai's cherished dining destination for over three decades. Born from a passion for authentic Indian cuisine, we bring the finest tandoori preparations, coastal seafood, and aromatic biryanis to your table.

Every dish is crafted with spices sourced from local farms and cooked by chefs who carry decades of culinary heritage. Our banana-leaf platters, clay oven kebabs, and slow-cooked gravies have earned us the loyalty of generations of food lovers.
